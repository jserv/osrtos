# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 12, 2026

## Introduction

OSRTOS ("we", "our", or "us") operates osrtos.com, a static content website providing information about open source real-time operating systems (RTOS), embedded libraries, and projects. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website.

## Information We Collect

### Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects information such as:

- Pages you visit and time spent on each page
- Your general geographic location (country/city level)
- Browser type and version
- Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
- Referring website or source
- Date and time of your visit

This data is collected anonymously and does not personally identify you.

### Technical Information

As a static website, we do not collect, store, or process any personal information directly. We do not have:

- User accounts or registration
- User databases
- Contact forms that store data on our servers
- Comments or user-generated content

## How We Use Information

The analytics data we collect through Google Analytics is used solely to:

- Understand website traffic patterns
- Improve website content and user experience
- Identify popular content and topics
- Make informed decisions about website development

We do not use this information for advertising, tracking across websites, or any other purpose.

## Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data. These cookies:

- Are placed by Google Analytics, not directly by us
- Do not contain personally identifiable information
- Expire according to Google's policies (typically 2 years)

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

## Third-Party Services

### Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to analyze website usage.

- [Google Analytics Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google Analytics Terms of Service](https://marketingplatform.google.com/about/analytics/terms/us/)
- [How Google Uses Data](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### External Links

Our website contains links to external websites (GitHub repositories, documentation sites, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites.

## Data Security

As a static website, we do not store or process personal data on our servers. All analytics data is handled by Google Analytics according to their security practices.

## Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data:

- **Access**: Request information about data collected
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics tracking using browser settings or the Google Analytics opt-out add-on

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were last made. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by opening an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/yplam/osrtos).

## Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with general privacy regulations including GDPR and CCPA, though as a static content website with minimal data collection, most provisions do not apply to our operations.
